Objective

Create a controlled two-page HCP leave-behind that reinforces the representative discussion after a Respivara call. The material must help HCPs recall patient-selection criteria, approved evidence context, safety considerations, and local prescribing-information access. It must not function as patient-facing material and must not introduce unsupported efficacy, speed, comparative, or durability claims.

Audience, Trigger, and Distribution Plan

AreaPlanned valueControl
AudienceConsented pulmonologists and high-prescribing GPs in India who receive an in-person or remote representative call.Distribution limited to approved HCP accounts in Veeva CRM; no patient or public use.
TriggerRepresentative follow-up after patient-selection discussion or HCP request for approved summary material.CRM call record must capture asset ID, date, representative, and HCP account.
FormatTwo-page PDF, A4 print-ready, optional tablet view, QR to local PI and approved HCP resource.Bleed, trim, safe area, QR scan, and PDF/A proof required before publish.
Success measuresHCP follow-up request rate, rep completion rate, QR scan rate, no MLR rework after first print proof.Metrics tied to CRM campaign object and post-distribution audit event.

Required Source Packet

  • DOC-CLAIM-RESP-008: approved patient-selection and safety-adjacent claim variants.
  • DOC-SMPC-RESP-2026: indication, population, warnings, and prescribing-information anchors.
  • DOC-CSR-RESP-P3-118: clinical evidence context, endpoint limitations, and statistics notes.
  • DOC-SAF-RESP-022: approved safety statement and fair-balance placement rules.
  • DOC-VAL-RESP-LB-073: HCP leave-behind validation matrix, KPIs, and test cases.
  • DOC-QA-RESP-LB-074: PDF proof, QR, print, accessibility, and distribution QA evidence.
