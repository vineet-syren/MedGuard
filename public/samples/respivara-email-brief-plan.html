Respivara HCP Approved Email Brief and Plan

Business Objective

Deliver a concise HCP-approved email that helps Indian pulmonologists and high-prescribing GPs identify eligible adult respiratory patients for an evidence-grounded Respivara discussion. The email must not introduce unsupported efficacy, speed, superiority, comparative, or durability claims. Every benefit statement must be traceable to the approved Respivara claim library and must carry adjacent safety and prescribing-information context.

Mandatory Validation Bundle

  • Campaign brief: objective, audience, claims, channel, owners, timing, success metrics, and handoff decisions.
  • Approved email copy deck: subject variants, preheader, hero, claim module, safety footer, CTA, PI link, and metadata.
  • Claim and evidence appendix: approved claim IDs, reference IDs, endpoint qualifiers, limitations, and expiry.
  • Safety and prescribing-information pack: mandatory safety text, placement rule, local PI destination, and fair-balance control.
  • Veeva CRM metadata pack: campaign code, consent flags, suppression timestamp, UTM map, owner matrix, and launch window.
  • Litmus QA report: rendering, link, accessibility, spam, tracking, and mobile checks.
